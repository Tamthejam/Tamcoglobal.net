How To Play - Escape The Dungeons

To Move The Character In Escape The Dungeons Use The Arrow Keys, Not WASD. To Kill A Scientist Hit Them On The Top Of The Head, If You Do Not Hit The Top Then You Will Lose 1 HP, You Have 3 HP. Same Rules Go For The Robots, Except If You Hit A Certan Arrow Key (You Will Have To Find Which One) They Will Shoot Out A Scientest. To Collect A Coin Touch It With Your Character And The Bottom Right Corner Score Will Increase. (Only On Latest Versions) If You Use The New Ruins Map Press "M" To Use The Map! The Greatest Rule: Have Fun!

How To Play - Air Attackers

To Play Air Attackers Use The Arrow Keys. If You Touch The Floor You Die! Press Down To Fire!

How To Play - Chef Guiseppe

Use The Same Controls As Escape The Dungeons. If Chef Guiseppe Touches You You Die! He will Follow You So Be Careful! Hold The Enter Key To Use Your Blaster! Reach The End To Proceed To The Nest Level!

How To Play - Escape The Dungeons 2

To Play Escape The Dungeons 2, One Player Uses Arrow Keys, To Move The Boy, And One Uses WASD To Move The Girl! If You Want To Do One Player You Must Use The Boy (Sorry Girls) If Either Of Them Die You Restart
